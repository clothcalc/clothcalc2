Merchandisers have an important job that involves doing complicated math. They have to remember lots of formulas, which can be hard sometimes. To make things easier, they often use Excel files. These files have pre-made formulas that they can use to do their calculations quickly. They just have to put in the right numbers, and Excel does the rest.

I found Excel really helpful for doing calculations, but not everyone can use it easily. So, I had an idea to make a website that would let more people do similar calculations without any trouble. To make this idea real, I had to do a lot of research.

During my research, I gained knowledge about various aspects of merchandising, such as calculating complex equations and using formulas. I also explored ways to simplify these calculations and present the results effectively. This research allowed me to discover innovative methods to streamline the process and make it easier to understand.

In conclusion, my journey from realizing the problems with traditional methods to coming up with the idea for a website and actually making it has been tough but satisfying. It's not just helped me learn new things, but also given others a useful tool for doing their calculations.
